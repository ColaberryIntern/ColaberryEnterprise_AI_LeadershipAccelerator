2Pathway-stage chip — navigational, not taxonomic

The chip used the same uppercase letter-spaced badge style as the trust-label badge. Operator and reviewer alike could read them as sibling category tags. The refinement: drop the background fill, drop the uppercase, drop the letter-spacing, and prepend a leading · that continues the title row's existing dot-separator rhythm.

Before: COORDINATION (uppercase, muted background, letter-spaced)
After: · Coordination (text-only, joins the row's editorial fragments)
