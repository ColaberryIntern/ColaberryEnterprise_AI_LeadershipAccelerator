Content vs activities · visibility new model — Ali 2026-06-26
Content · always open

Course, NotebookLM video, readings & articles. Open across every section, any time — never locked, never hidden. Marked with an "Open" pill.

Activities · before you start

Lab, record & critique, post-quiz, survey, interview are hidden behind a "reveal once you start" prompt. The content above stays usable.

Activities · after first activity

Complete one activity and the rest reveal in order, each gated by its prerequisite (lab → post-quiz → interview).

Visibility = timeline

If it isn't visible here, it can't appear in the main timeline. The "today" feed only pulls items currently visible to that student — hidden activities stay out until revealed.

Switch the week picker to a future week on the live screen to see the "before you start" state — content open, activities behind the reveal prompt.

System states
Course link unconfirmed

If a week's Skilljar URL is pending (not yet confirmed by Kes), the course card shows a muted "Course link coming soon" state and the rest of the week stays usable.

Video not yet produced

NotebookLM video for a future week shows a "Drops on {date}" placeholder rather than a dead player.

Offline / link error

Deep-link failure surfaces a retry + a copyable URL fallback — never a silent dead end (failure-first).

Mobile (< 980px)

Sidebar collapses under the main column; the step rail stays as a vertical checklist. Single-column, thumb-reachable actions.

Visibility model (Ali 2026-06-26) — drives the timeline. Content (course, video, readings) is always visible/open across all sections. Activities (lab, record, quiz, survey, interview) are hidden until the student starts the section and completes ≥1 activity, then reveal in prerequisite order. Build rule: the student's main timeline / "today" feed is derived from currently-visible items only — an item not visible on the week page MUST NOT be emitted to the timeline. A single visible / revealed_at flag per (enrollment_id, section, item) is the source of truth for both the week page and the timeline.
Locked decision honored (BC#9985688697): Skilljar = deep-link v1. Open in a new tab; we link/wrap, we do not re-host. SSO + embed + automatic progress-mirror are P1, conditional on Anthropic Partner enablement. The screen must not assume a progress callback exists.
Per-student isolation, idempotency & failure-first
ConcernRequirement
IsolationEvery read/write scoped to enrollment_id + project_id. A student only ever sees their own quiz attempts, interview sessions, recordings, lab artifacts, and progress. Auth on every route + resource-ownership check.
IdempotencyQuiz submit, lab score, artifact file, and "complete week" are keyed (enrollment_id, week, item) — re-submitting never double-awards points or files a duplicate artifact.
Failure-firstSkilljar deep-link open is fire-and-forget with a copyable URL fallback. Video/asset load has a placeholder + retry. No external call blocks the page; timeouts + capped retries on any progress fetch (P1).
DeterminismPoints, week %, and readiness lift are pure functions over completion events — never LLM-derived. Same events ⇒ same score.
