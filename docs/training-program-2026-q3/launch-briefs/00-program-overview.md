## What we are building

**AI Systems Architect Accelerator** — a 12-week, project-based, AI-systems-architect training program. Career changers and working professionals arrive with their own venture idea; the platform turns that idea into a 12-week build; they graduate with a working AI system, a GitHub portfolio, the Claude Certified Architect — Foundations cert, and ongoing community + consulting opportunities.

- **Brand:** AI Systems Architect Accelerator
- **Powered by:** Anthropic + Claude Code (Partner Network co-branding once partner status confirmed 2026-06-12)
- **One-line:** "Bring your idea. We help you turn it into a real AI system."
- **Positioning:** "Learn from Anthropic. Build through Colaberry. Deploy in the real world."

**Reject these names:** Prompt Engineering / AI Bootcamp / Learn Claude / ChatGPT Training.

**Differentiation:** Anthropic certifies *knowledge*. Colaberry certifies *capability*. Every student leaves with a unique project, not an identical capstone. Project-Builder-first model — the project IS the curriculum generator.

## How the 12 weeks are structured

12 weeks total. **Mon Architecture Day (2hr) + Thu Build Day (2hr).** Sold as 4 stackable 3-week Architect Intensives at $499 each (TWC compliance); bundled at $1,497.

| # | Market name | Tech name | Weeks |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Build Your AI Foundation | Claude Code Foundations | 1–3 |
| 2 | Create Your AI Team | Agent Engineering | 4–6 |
| 3 | Connect AI To The Real World | MCP & Enterprise Integration | 7–9 |
| 4 | Design AI That Scales | AI Systems Architecture | 10–12 |
| (opt) | Applied AI Consulting Lab | Apprentice on real Colaberry projects | post-grad |

**One coherent system across 12 weeks** (Lego model — each intensive builds on the last). The TWC seminar-independence test is handled in `06-twc-context.md`.
