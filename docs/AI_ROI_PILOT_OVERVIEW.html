The offer

Low barrier in, flexible upside out. The pilot is the foot in the door; the deal that follows is the business.

$2,500
6-week AI ROI Pilot. 100% credited toward whatever we build next. One real working system, not slideware.
Monthly retainer — predictable fee to keep building and running. Best when they have a backlog of ideas.
Revenue share — lower fixed fee plus a share of revenue the system generates. The structure Percy asked for.
Pay per outcome — a set fee per result, e.g. each new booked client. Pure performance.
