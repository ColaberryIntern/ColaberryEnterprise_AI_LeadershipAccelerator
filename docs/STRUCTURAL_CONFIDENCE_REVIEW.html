Structural Confidence + Operational Maturity Expression — Sprint Review

Shipped 2026-05-15 to enterprise.colaberry.ai (commits 2a21689 + a90d230). The classifier already knew whether a domain was Foundational, Emerging, Coordinated, Operational, Scaling, or Stabilizing. This sprint translates those technical states into operator-facing confidence language and adds one calm editorial sentence per domain about how that area feels operationally.

The same structural facts, a softer reading. "Foundational" becomes "Still forming" on the badge. "Emerging" becomes "Coordinating". Below the narrative, an italic confidence line says what the operator can sense without reading the technical state at all. What this sprint explicitly is NOT: monitoring software, analytics, KPI dashboards, red/yellow/green enterprise tooling, health-score scoring, telemetry surfacing. The lifecycle state machine and every other piece of computation is exactly as it was. Only the surface expression softened.
