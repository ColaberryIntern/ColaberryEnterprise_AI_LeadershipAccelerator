# 2. The Problem

Enterprise AI adoption is stalling — not because of technology, but because of execution.

**The patterns we see repeatedly:**

- **AI initiatives stall at the strategy phase.** Organizations spend 6-12 months on assessments and strategy decks that never translate to deployed systems.

- **Over-reliance on consultants creates dependency.** External firms build systems your team can't maintain, modify, or scale — creating a permanent cost center instead of internal capability.

- **Leadership lacks deployment fluency.** Executives can articulate AI vision but can't evaluate build-vs-buy decisions, assess vendor claims, or direct technical teams with confidence.

- **Time-to-value is unacceptable.** The average enterprise AI project takes 12-18 months from concept to production. Competitors deploying in weeks, not quarters, are pulling ahead.

- **Governance is an afterthought.** Teams rush to deploy without compliance frameworks, creating legal and reputational risk that forces costly rework.

The cost of these patterns is not just budget — it's competitive position, executive credibility, and organizational momentum.

# 10. Frequently Asked Questions

**Do participants need technical skills?**
No. This program is designed for business and technology leaders, not data scientists. Participants need to understand their business problems and data landscape — we handle the technical translation. That said, participants with technical backgrounds will go deeper on architecture and implementation details.

**How is proprietary data handled?**
Participants can use anonymized or synthetic data during the program. All work is conducted under NDA. No proprietary data is stored, shared, or used beyond the participant's own work. Governance and data handling protocols are established in Week 1.

**What if we're early in our AI adoption journey?**
This program is particularly effective for organizations at the beginning of their AI journey. Starting with execution rather than strategy means you build real capability from Day 1 instead of spending months on assessments. The readiness scan in Week 1 meets you where you are.

**What if we miss this cohort?**
Cohorts run on a regular schedule. However, each cohort has limited seats to maintain quality of expert-guided interaction. We recommend securing your spot early — the next available cohort may be 4-8 weeks out.

**Can we send participants from different departments?**
Yes — and we encourage it. Cross-functional teams (e.g., technology + operations + strategy) create stronger organizational alignment and accelerate post-program deployment. Team Sponsorship pricing reflects this.

**What's the time commitment?**
The program runs over 3 weeks with structured sessions and guided build time. Expect 8-10 hours per week. Sessions are designed for working executives — no full-day workshops that pull leaders out of their operations.
