Beats and channels

A quick reference for the theme and call to action behind each beat, and where each channel does its best work. No price appears anywhere in awareness copy. Pricing is revealed at the Open House.

Beat 1 The shift is already happening

Set the frame. Most people use AI, very few build with it. CTA. Reserve your free seat.

Beat 2 From AI user to AI builder

Turn the idea into a possibility the reader can picture. CTA. Register for the Open House.

Beat 3 See what you will build

Show the 12 weeks. Bring your idea, leave with a working AI system. CTA. Reserve your seat.

Beat 4 Meet the founding cohort

Scarcity with warmth. Only 40 seats available. CTA. Register before seats fill.

Beat 5 Final reminder

We are live today. Last chance to join us. CTA. Add to calendar.

Locked Drive to the Open House

Every post and email points to the free registration. No price, no em-dashes, warm and credible voice throughout.

Where each channel earns its place

Lean in

  • LinkedIn is primary. It reaches alumni and working professionals.
  • X carries the punchy, short angle that sparks replies.
  • Instagram does the visual lift. Carousel for Beat 3, stories for urgency.
  • Email is the parallel owned channel running the approved five-email sequence.

Keep out

  • No price in any awareness or outbound copy. It is revealed at the Open House.
  • No em-dash characters. Use periods and commas.
  • No emoji anywhere.
  • No invented seat counts. If a number appears, it is 40 founding seats.

How to use this

This calendar is the operating layer for the campaign. Read it alongside the five-beat sequence and the campaign brief.

Confirm the open tokens

Fill [reg link] and [time + TZ] from Basecamp 9946499609 before the first send.

Decide on the teaser

Run the July 2 teaser only if you want to warm the audience early. It is optional and faded on the calendar for that reason.

Let the engine generate at send time

Each segment campaign has its own prompt. Posts and emails are generated when they send, with baked-in rules. No price, no em-dashes, drive to the Open House.

Watch registrations and adjust

Registrations return through the Open House modal as open_house leads. If pace lags, tighten the spacing of Beats 3 through 5. Dates are adjustable.
