Goal and the single funnel

One objective drives every post, email, and asset. We generate free Open House registrations, then convert attendees into Founding Cohort enrollment.

The goal

Generate free Open House registrations for Thursday, July 16, 2026, then convert attendees into Founding Cohort enrollment for the Cohort 1 kickoff on Thursday, July 23, 2026. The Open House precedes the cohort it feeds by one week.

The program

AI Systems Architect Accelerator, powered by Anthropic and Claude Code. A 12-week, implementation-focused program that moves working professionals, career changers, builders, and technical professionals from AI consumer to AI builder. Learn with Claude. Build through Colaberry. Deploy in the real world.

One funnel, two conversions

Conversion 1 is the free registration. Conversion 2 is enrollment at the Open House. Pricing is held until the Open House, so every outbound message sells the free seat, not the price.

How the segments power the engine

The Accelerator runs a native, prompt-based campaign engine. Each segment campaign has its own ai_system_prompt, and posts and emails are generated at send time. Baked-in rules: no price, no em-dashes, drive to the Open House.

Risks and flags

The watch-outs that can break this campaign. Clear these before assets ship.

Pricing must stay out of outbound

No price in social posts, captions, the calendar, sequences, or email subject and body. Price is revealed at the Open House. Reference docs like this brief may state it, clearly labeled.

Seat count: use 40, not 25

An earlier email draft said 25 founding seats. That is superseded. If a seat count appears anywhere, it must be 40.

Kill the stale 6/21 date

Any reference to a 6/21 date is stale. The Open House is Thursday, July 16, 2026. Scrub 6/21 from every asset before launch.
