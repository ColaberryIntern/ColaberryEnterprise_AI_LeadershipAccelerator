Logo use & inversion

The mark adapts to its background by flipping its foreground. The cherry element never moves off-brand.

Inversion rule

On light surfaces, the wordmark foreground is dark. On dark or photo surfaces, the foreground inverts to light. In both states the cherry mark stays exactly #FB2832. The foreground inverts. The cherry is constant.

Keep clear space around the mark equal to the height of the cherry element. Do not place the logo on busy imagery without a solid or scrim panel behind it.

Never recolor the mark
Do not tint the cherry element leaf or berry, do not gradient it, do not outline it, and do not apply effects. If a background fights the mark, change the background or add a panel.

Data-viz palette

Charts use the shared series tokens, not the brand ramps. This keeps every chart legible and consistent.

Use --chart-1 through --chart-8
Pull series colors from the CSS tokens --chart-1 .. --chart-8 via getComputedStyle. Never hardcode cherry, leaf, or berry as chart series. Brand colors are for brand moments, not for stacked bars where adjacent fills must stay distinguishable. Pair every series with a label or legend so meaning never relies on color alone.

Accessibility rules

Readable first. These rules are not optional and apply to every asset in the kit.

Body text colors

  • Reading copy uses the ink and muted text tokens on light surfaces, light tokens on dark surfaces.
  • Do not set small body text in raw cherry or raw leaf. Those colors fail contrast at body sizes.
  • Reserve cherry and leaf for headings, accents, fills, and large display where contrast holds.

Links, focus, and signals

  • Links are berry blue (#367895), underlined or clearly distinguished, never color-only.
  • Every interactive element keeps a visible focus ring. Do not remove focus outlines.
  • Never rely on color alone. Pair status color with a word, icon, or label.
  • Maintain comfortable body size. No small body text squeezed below a readable minimum.
Berry blue is the reading-safe accent
When you need an accent inside or near body text, reach for berry blue. It holds contrast where cherry and leaf do not.

Do / Don't

The fast checklist every asset must pass before it leaves the building.

Do

  • Keep cherry red constant at #FB2832 across every surface.
  • Use Roboto for display and body, Roboto Mono for metrics and code.
  • Invert the logo foreground for dark backgrounds, keep the cherry mark unchanged.
  • Pull chart series from --chart-1 .. --chart-8 and label every series.
  • Make links berry blue and keep visible focus rings.
  • Write in sentence case, warm and credible, speaking to you.
  • Use RemixIcon glyphs for any iconography.

Don't

  • No emoji anywhere. Use RemixIcon icons instead.
  • Do not recolor, gradient, outline, or add effects to the mark.
  • No Inter, Arial, or other font substitutes for Roboto or Roboto Mono.
  • Do not set small body text in raw cherry or raw leaf.
  • Do not use brand ramps as chart series colors.
  • Do not remove focus outlines or rely on color alone for meaning.
  • Do not use em-dash characters. Use periods and commas.
