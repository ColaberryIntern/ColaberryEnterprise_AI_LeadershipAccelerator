What the Open House is, and why it matters

A free, live event that opens the door to the AI Systems Architect Accelerator, powered by Anthropic and Claude Code. It is the front of the funnel for the Founding Cohort.

The Open House is your one-hour invitation to meet the program before you commit. We show working professionals, career changers, builders, and technical professionals what it means to move from AI consumer to AI builder. The promise we lead with is simple: learn with Claude, build through Colaberry, deploy in the real world.

What it is

A free, live walkthrough of the 12-week, implementation-focused Accelerator. Attendees see the four-phase journey, hear the AI user versus AI builder story, and learn how the Founding Cohort works.

Why it matters

It is the only event that feeds Cohort 1. Most people are consuming AI. Very few are learning how to build with it. The Open House is where we make that gap real and show the path across it.

What happens next

Attendees become the warm audience for Founding Cohort enrollment. The Open House precedes the cohort it feeds by one week, so momentum carries straight into kickoff.

The proof we lead with

Warm, inclusive, and proven since 2012, with 5,000 plus careers launched. The Open House is where new builders see that track record before they decide.

Keep this an awareness doc

No price appears anywhere in this playbook or in any outbound copy it drives. Pricing is revealed at the Open House itself. Every post, caption, and email drives to the free registration, never to a number.

Do

  • Drive every post and email to the free Open House.
  • Lead with proof and the user-versus-builder story.
  • Keep voice warm, credible, sentence case, speaking to you.
  • Let the campaign engine generate copy at send time per segment.

Don't

  • Never state a price in any outbound copy. Price is revealed at the Open House.
  • No emoji and no em-dash characters anywhere.
  • Do not invent metrics, names, quotes, or seat counts. The cap is 40.
  • Do not break the beat order, even if dates move.

How the copy gets made

The Accelerator runs a native, prompt-based campaign engine. Each segment campaign carries its own ai_system_prompt, and posts and emails are generated at send time. Baked-in rules: no price, no em-dashes, and always drive to the Open House.

How registrations come back

Links carry UTM parameters and a campaign_id off env.openHouseLandingUrl. The OpenHouseModal posts to /api/leads, and each registration lands as an open_house_ lead, tagged to the campaign that drove it.

Why the tagging matters

Because every lead is stamped with its source campaign, the team can see which beat and channel filled the room, and can follow up with attendees to convert them into the 40-seat Founding Cohort.
