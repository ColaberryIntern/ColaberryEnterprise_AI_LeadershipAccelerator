One spine, two channels

The approved five-email sequence is the spine. Social runs the exact same five beats, in the same order, on the same week, so a person who sees a LinkedIn post and then opens an email feels one continuous story.

Email is the owned channel that converts: it lands in the inbox, makes the ask, and counts the registration. Social is the earned and paid channel that seeds: it puts the idea into the feed, builds familiarity, and warms people up before the email arrives. We do not run two campaigns. We run one campaign through two channels.

Email converts

The five-beat sequence is the owned channel. Each email makes one ask and drives to the Open House registration. Registrations return as open_house_* leads.

Why beat alignment matters
When the social post and the email share a beat in the same week, the second touch feels familiar instead of new. Familiarity lowers the cost of the ask, so when email makes the registration request the audience has already met the idea in the feed.

Social seeds, email converts

The rhythm of the campaign. Social runs a day or two ahead of each email so the feed warms the audience first, then the inbox makes the ask. Both build to the free Open House on July 16.

Social goes first

The matching social beat posts a day or two before its email. It plants the idea in the feed where no commitment is required, building familiarity at low cost.

Email follows

The email lands while the social beat is still fresh. Because the idea is already familiar, the email can move straight to the ask: reserve a free seat at the Open House.

Reinforcement loop

People who acted on social see the email as confirmation. People who only opened the email recognize the post when it cycles in the feed. Each touch reinforces the other.

Converge on Jul 16

Beat 5 fires on both channels the day of the Open House. Social runs a live-today countdown, email sends the final reminder, both pointing at the same free room.

Do

  • Run the matching social beat before its email, in the same week.
  • Keep both channels on the same five beats in the same order.
  • Drive every post and every email to the free Open House registration.
  • Keep all price out of awareness copy on both channels.

Don't

  • Don't let social and email tell different stories in the same week.
  • Don't post a price, a checkout link, or a discount anywhere.
  • Don't run a beat on one channel and skip it on the other.
  • Don't use emoji or em-dashes in any line of copy.

Where registrations go
Both channels send people to the Open House registration via env.openHouseLandingUrl with UTM and campaign_id. Sign-ups return through the OpenHouseModal to /api/leads as open_house_* leads, so social-seeded and email-converted registrations land in the same place.
