Approvals gate

These are the five items that hold the launch. Each one is a hard gate. Until it is resolved and signed off, the campaign stays off. Tokens render literally. Never invent a value.

Gate 1. Registration link [reg link]

[reg link] is the Open House registration URL from Basecamp 9946499609. It must be filled, tested end to end, and confirmed working before any asset that points to it can go out. Until then, leave the token in place rather than guessing a URL.

Gate 2. Start time [time + TZ]

[time + TZ] is the exact Open House start time and timezone. Beat 5, the calendar add, and the landing page all depend on it. Set it once, place it everywhere, and only then schedule the day-of sends.

Gate 3. Refund and cancellation terms

The refund and cancellation terms are drafted and pending Ali. They must be approved before enrollment is discussed at or after the Open House. Until approved, do not publish any terms language.

Gate 4. Fix the seat count, 25 to 40

An earlier email draft said 25 seats. That is superseded. Every reference must read 40 founding seats. Scrub all assets, including Beat 4, before anything ships.

Gate 5. Scrub the stale 6/21 date

Some assets still carry June 21. The Open House is Thursday, July 16, 2026, and Cohort 1 kicks off Thursday, July 23. Remove every June 21 reference, including the landing page and any approved video concepts, before marketing ships.

Any open gate is a full stop

If even one of these five gates is unresolved, the campaign does not launch. There is no partial send. Clear all five, then return to the final go / no-go block below.

Go / no-go flow

The decision path in one picture. Every gate must pass. A single fail routes back to the owner and holds the send.

The launch never skips a gate
flowchart TD
  A["Pre-flight phases complete"] --> B{"All five gates Ready?"}
  B -- "No" --> H["Hold. Route back to owner"]
  H --> A
  B -- "Yes" --> C{"Ali final go?"}
  C -- "No" --> H
  C -- "Yes" --> D["Confirm reg link and time live"]
  D --> E["Send Beat 1 to Beat 5"]
  E --> F["Open House Jul 16"]
  F --> G["Convert to Founding Cohort"]

Green means all green

The flow only reaches a send when both checks pass. Gates Ready, then Ali final go. Any No on either check loops back to the owner and the campaign waits.

Final go / no-go

Run these steps in order on launch morning. Do not start step two until step one is true. The last step is the only thing that turns the campaign on.

Confirm every gate reads Ready

Walk the five approval gates above. [reg link] live, [time + TZ] set, refund terms approved, seat count at 40, stale 6/21 scrubbed. If any is not Ready, stop here.

Verify tracking end to end

Submit a test registration. Confirm it lands as an open_house lead through the modal and that UTM and campaign_id are attached. Fix any gap before proceeding.

Lock the calendar and send windows

Confirm beat dates against the content calendar, decide on the optional teaser, and set send times for the audience and timezone.

Get the explicit go from Ali

Record Ali's final go in writing. This is the single approval that unlocks sending. No go, no send.

Enable the campaign

Turn on the segment campaigns. The engine generates each post and email at send time with the baked-in rules. No price, no em-dashes, drive to the Open House. Watch the first send land, then monitor registrations.

Nothing sends until the gates clear

This is the rule the whole page exists to protect. No social post, no email, no calendar invite goes out before all five gates are Ready and Ali has given the final go. When in doubt, hold.
