What it is and who it is for

The starting questions. Tap any question to open the grounded answer you can adapt and paste.

What is the AI Systems Architect Accelerator?

It is a 12-week, implementation-focused program, powered by Anthropic and Claude Code, that moves you from AI consumer to AI builder. You learn with Claude, build through Colaberry, and deploy in the real world. By the end you have a working AI system, not just notes.

Who is this for?

It is built for working professionals, career changers, builders, and technical professionals who want to stop just using AI and start building with it. Most people are consuming AI. Very few are learning how to build with it. This program is for the people who want to be in that second group.

I'm an alum. Is this for me?

Yes. You've already invested in your future. Now learn how to build with AI. The Accelerator is the natural next step for our community, and the Open House is free, so you can see exactly how it fits before deciding.

I started a Colaberry program before but didn't finish. Can I come back?

Absolutely. Your journey doesn't have to end here. This is a new opportunity built for today's AI economy. Come to the free Open House and see what building with AI looks like now.

Proof point you can use

5,000+ careers since 2012. Warm, inclusive, and proven since 2012. This is approved and may appear in your replies.

Do I need to code, and prerequisites

The single most common DM. Keep the answer welcoming and honest.

Do I need to know how to code?

This is an implementation-focused program where you build and deploy on real, live AI tools through Claude Code. If you have a technical background you will move fast. If you are newer to building, you are still welcome, and the program is structured to take you from foundation to deployment over 12 weeks. The Open House is the best place to ask about your exact starting point and what to expect.

What do I need before I start?

You build on real tools, so you will need an Anthropic subscription for Claude Code and pay for your own LLM API usage. These are disclosed before enrollment and paid directly to the providers. The full list of what to prepare is covered at the Open House. See the cost section below for the third-party amounts.

If you are not sure whether you qualify

Do not guess thresholds we have not published. Invite the person to the free Open House where they can ask about their background directly. That is always a safe, warm answer.

How long is it, and what will I build

12 weeks, four phases. Bring your idea, leave with a working AI system.

How long is the program and how is it structured?

It runs 12 weeks across four phases that take you from a foundation to a system that scales:

Weeks 1 to 3, Build Your AI Foundation

Get grounded in building with Claude and the core workflow.

Weeks 4 to 6, Create Your AI Team

Move from single prompts to coordinated AI agents.

Weeks 7 to 9, Connect AI To The Real World

Wire your system into real tools, data, and deployment.

Weeks 10 to 12, Design AI That Scales

Harden, scale, and earn the Anthropic Architect certification.

What will I actually build?

You build and deploy a real, working AI system on live AI tools. You start from a foundation, move from single prompts to coordinated AI agents, connect your system to real tools and data, then harden and scale it. The short version we use on social: bring your idea, leave with a working AI system.
