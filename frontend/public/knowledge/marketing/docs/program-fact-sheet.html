What the program is

A 12-week, implementation-focused program that moves working professionals, career changers, builders, and technical professionals from AI consumer to AI builder.

Colaberry has launched more than 5,000 careers since 2012. The brand is warm, inclusive, and proven since 2012. That proof point is approved for use. The positioning line is: learn with Claude, build through Colaberry, deploy in the real world.

The 4-phase curriculum

Twelve weeks, four phases, three weeks each. Each phase moves you one step closer to a working, deployed AI system.

Weeks 1 to 3 · Build your AI foundation

You start with the fundamentals of building, not just prompting. By the end of phase one you understand how to work with Claude as a true building partner.

Weeks 4 to 6 · Create your AI team

You learn to design and coordinate multiple AI agents that work together, so your system does more than answer one question at a time.

Weeks 7 to 9 · Connect AI to the real world

You wire your system into real tools, data, and services. This is where your project stops being a demo and starts doing real work.

Weeks 10 to 12 · Design AI that scales

You harden, deploy, and scale what you built. You leave with a working AI system and the Anthropic Architect certification.

Student-paid third-party costs

This is a required disclosure. Share it before enrollment. These costs are paid directly to the providers and are not covered by Colaberry.

You pay two small third-party costs, directly to the providers

Because you build and deploy on real, live AI tools, you will need an Anthropic subscription for Claude Code at about $20 per month and LLM API usage, most projects under $10 per month. These are paid directly to the providers and are not part of the Colaberry tuition. Disclose this before anyone enrolls.

Claude Code subscription

About $20 per month, paid to Anthropic. This gives you the building environment the program is taught in.

LLM API usage

Most projects run under $10 per month in API usage, paid to the provider. Cost scales with what you build.

Why these costs exist

This is an implementation-focused program. You build and deploy on real, live AI tools rather than simulated ones, so a working tool account and live API usage are required. That is what lets you leave with a real, deployed system.
