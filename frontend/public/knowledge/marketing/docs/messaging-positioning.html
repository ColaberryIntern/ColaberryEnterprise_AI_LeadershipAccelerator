Voice: do and don't

Warm and credible. Sentence case. Speak to "you". Lead with proof. Friendly CTAs. This block is the quick check before anything ships.

Do

  • Write in sentence case across body copy and captions.
  • Speak directly to "you" and keep it warm.
  • Lead with proof: 12 weeks, build real systems, since 2012.
  • Use friendly, specific CTAs like reserve your free seat.
  • Drive every awareness asset to the free Open House.
  • Use periods and commas where you would reach for a dash.
  • Keep claims to the approved facts on this page.

Don't

  • No emoji anywhere, in any channel.
  • No em-dash characters. They are baked out of the engine.
  • No price in awareness or outbound copy. See the pricing rule.
  • No invented claims, stats, testimonials, names, or quotes.
  • No ALL CAPS shouting or hypey "guru" language.
  • No vague CTAs like learn more with nowhere to go.
  • No abbreviating the program name in headlines.
